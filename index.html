Our Ministries

PCMF

PCMF is a men's fellowship group with a clarion call to attract, lead, and develop men to Christ.
It aims at transforming men through Jesus Christ

Read More

Youth Ministry

This is a group which serves teens aged 13-19 and youth aged 20- 35. The group is responsible for their spiritual growth, mentorship and guidance through social, economic and spiritual programmes.

Read More

Children Ministry


The Children ministry is a ministry whose goal is to teach liturgy and provide guidance to children aged between 2 and 14 years old.
This ministry offers holistic education (both home and church school) to children on Sundays during service hours. In addition, it has various activities throughout the year which promote growth and cohesion among children.
